Enlarging the views

Sometimes the Formula View pane is not wide enough to display the entire tree view. In Excel for Windows or the Mac, we can hover the cursor over its left edge and drag it left to widen the pane.

Widening the Formula View pane

Undocking the views

In Excel for Windows, we can make the Formula View pane even wider by dragging its top edge left to undock the pane and then resizing the undocked pane by dragging its edges outward.
