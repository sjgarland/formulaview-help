Use Cases

The following sample uses of Formula Forge demonstrate its capabilities.

  • Recognizing a formula is made easier by the math view.
  • Dealing with parentheses is also made easier by the math view.
  • Debugging a formula is made easier by the tree view.
  • Examining a long formula is made easier by being able to collapse and expand elements in the tree view.
