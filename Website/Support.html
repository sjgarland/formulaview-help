Support

For general information about Formula Forge, send email to info@formulaforge.com.

To report a problem with Formula Forge or its documentation, send email to support@formulaforge.com. Please tell us which version of Excel you are using. If you are using Excel for the web, please tell us also which browser you are using. (You can find version and browser information by clicking About in the Formula Forge menu.)

If you would like to request an enhancement to Formula Forge, please let us know. If your request seems generally useful, and we can figure out how to fulfill it, we will do our best to oblige.
