About

Stage One Software creates tools for technical professionals to help analyze and model their data and make better business decisions. A partnership of three highly experienced technical practitioners, Stage One Software is founded on their extensive experience using spreadsheet programs in all aspects of business from technical analysis and performance modeling to financial reporting and projections to database applications.

Stage One Software’s products enable users to see formulas from multiple points of view, thereby helping them find and correct errors in complicated expressions. Its patented method of synchronizing views ensures that all views are displayed consistently, correlated, and updated when changes are made. Stage One Software’s products improve the productivity of knowledgeable people by streamlining spreadsheet development and debugging.
