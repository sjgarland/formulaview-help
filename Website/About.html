About Us

Stage One Software creates tools for technical professionals to help analyze and model their data and make better business decisions. A partnership of three highly experienced technical practitioners, Stage One Software is founded on their extensive experience using spreadsheet programs in all aspects of business from technical analysis and performance modeling to financial reporting and projections to database applications.

Stage One's products improve the productivity of knowledgeable people by streamlining spreadsheet development and debugging. Its primary product, Formula Forge, helps Excel users find and correct errors in complicated formulas. US Patent 8510650B2, Multiple synchronized views for creating, analyzing, editing, and using mathematical formulas, protects the design and implementation of Formula Forge.
