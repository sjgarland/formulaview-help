Enlarging the Formula View pane

Sometimes the Formula View pane is not wide enough to display the entire tree view. In Excel for Windows or the Mac, users can hover the cursor over its left edge and drag it left to widen the pane.

Widening the Formula View pane

Undocking the Formula View pane

In Excel for Windows, users can make the Formula View pane even wider by dragging its top edge left to undock the pane and then resizing the undocked pane by dragging its edges outward.

Undocking the Formula View pane
Enlarging the undocked pane

Creating a floating Formula View canvas

Excel for the Mac limits the width of an undocked Formula View pane, and Excel for the web does not allow any increase in the width of the pane, nor does it allow the pane to be undocked. Hence a menu item in these versions of Formula View allows users to display Formula View in a floating canvas with no limits on its width.
