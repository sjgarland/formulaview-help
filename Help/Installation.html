Installation and Terms of Use

Installation

When Formula Forge becomes available from Microsoft's AppSource market place, click Get Add-Ins on Excel's Insert ribbon, search for Formula Forge in the Microsoft store, and click its Add button in the list of search results. Prior to then, users who wish to experiment with Formula Forge can request and install a preliminary release.
